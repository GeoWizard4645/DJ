Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to rent speakers, or do you bring them?

You don't need to worry about the tech! My standard quotes include the option for speaker and equipment rentals tailored to the size of your crowd. If you choose that option, I bring the FLX4 controller, powered speakers, stands, cables, and a mic.

Prices seem a bit high, but I want a fun event. Can you help?

Absolutely! If you're looking for a low-commitment, fun event that's a massive step up from just playing a Spotify playlist on a Bluetooth speaker, contact me. As a high schooler doing this out of passion, I am open to negotiations and we can easily work out a cheaper pricing structure depending on your exact needs and how much heavy equipment is required. Let's make it work for your budget!

Do you take song requests?

Yes! I love working with clients to build a baseline playlist of must-hears and do-not-plays. During the event, I'm happy to take live requests from the crowd as long as they fit the overall vibe you want for the night.

What genres do you play?

Pop and Bollywood are my absolute specialties. However, as an experienced musician, I read the room well and I am completely open to DJing whatever genres—Hip-Hop, EDM, R&B, Throwbacks—make your party unforgettable.

Why does an event require so much prep time?

Prep time isn't just throwing together a single playlist—it's building the architecture of your entire event. I meticulously set up 8-12 different modular playlists (which I call "waves") tailored to different energy levels, crowd ages, and different amounts of people on the dance floor at any given time. Additionally, I analyze tracks for BPM, musical key, and vibe compatibility to guarantee seamless transitions, and I practice live mixing specific sequences. All this behind-the-scenes work ensures I can flawlessly read the room and keep the dance floor packed all night.
